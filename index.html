About Us

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, delivers and abundance of resources that advance computing as a science and a profession. ACM provide a range of activities and services including talks by local practitioners, visits from prominent speakers on the ACM Distinguished Speakers Program circuit, technical and career workshops. IOIT ACM is established in 2019 with a vision to promote the field of computing and to help shape an individual’s technical aspects. Be a member of our community.
